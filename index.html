About

Midnight Static is an electronic synth rock band based out of Boston, MA. Filled with layers of catchy melodies and sweeping chord changes, along with nuances of 70's Prog Rock, 80's New Wave and current electronic pop acts such as CHVRCHES and Cut Copy, the music of Midnight Static stirs up moments of nostalgia while hinting at the future of electronic dance rock.
